# BBXAI

**BBXAI** / **BB × AI** is an AI-assisted loopstation concept for beatboxers and vocal sound-makers.

The product goal is simple:

> Record human-made beatbox sounds. Let the system arrange, clean, balance, and structure them into music.

BBXAI is not meant to be a prompt-based AI music generator. It is an arranger and production assistant for sounds created by the user.

## First success condition

The first accepted version is not defined by a feature checklist.

The first accepted version is the moment the creator records their own beatbox sounds, hears the result, and feels:

> Wow. I can actually do this.

## Target user

BBXAI is for amateur beatbox enthusiasts, vocal sound-makers, and casual creators who can make interesting sounds but feel overwhelmed by DAWs, loopstations, music theory, effects chains, and traditional production tools.

## Human / AI boundary

Human-controlled:

- recorded sounds
- vibe and taste
- final approval
- performance identity
- creative source material

AI-assisted:

- arrangement
- timing cleanup
- sound placement
- EQ suggestions
- volume balancing
- effects suggestions
- section building

Core principle:

> The creativity is human. The structure is assisted.
