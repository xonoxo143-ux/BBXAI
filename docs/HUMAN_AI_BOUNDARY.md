# Human / AI Boundary

BBXAI should keep a clear boundary between human creativity and AI assistance.

## Must stay human

The following should stay human-controlled:

- recorded sounds
- vocal/beatbox technique
- vibe
- taste
- final approval
- performance identity
- creative source material

The user should always be able to say:

> Every sound in this track came from me.

## AI should assist with

The AI should mainly assist with:

- arrangement
- sound placement
- timing cleanup
- section building
- EQ suggestions
- volume balancing
- effects suggestions
- transition suggestions
- loop variation

## AI should not lead with

The AI should not primarily:

- invent new source sounds from nothing
- replace the user’s beatbox performance
- generate unrelated full songs
- hide what came from the user
- make the user feel like they merely prompted a machine

## Core principle

> The creativity is human. The structure is assisted.

## Product identity line

> BBXAI is not an AI music generator. It is an AI arranger for human-made beatbox sounds.
